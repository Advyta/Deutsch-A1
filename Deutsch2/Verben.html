Verben

German verbs are words that express actions, states, or occurrences in a sentence. They are essential components of German sentences and undergo changes to indicate tense, person, and number. Verbs can be categorized into regular and irregular verbs, depending on their conjugation patterns.


Verbkonjugationen

Das Verb= Verbstamm + Endung
Beispeil: wohnen= wohn + en


Types of Verbs

  • Unregelmäßige Verben
