Uregnelmäßige Verben

In German, irregular verbs can undergo changes in the stem (root) of the verb, or they may have entirely different forms for past tenses or the past participle. Because of these variations, irregular verbs need to be learned individually, as there are no fixed rules that apply to all of them.

Catagories:

  1. e-i
  2. e-ie
  3. a-ä
  4. au-äu

Verbkonjugationen

e-i

du hilfst nimmst sprichst triffst wirfst
er/sie/es hilft nihmmt spricht trifft wirft
wir helfen nehmen sprechen treffen werfen
